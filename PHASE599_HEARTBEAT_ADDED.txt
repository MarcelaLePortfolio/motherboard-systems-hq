Phase 599 system heartbeat endpoint added.

Purpose:
- Introduce a minimal, safe observability endpoint.
- No impact to existing execution pipeline or UI.

Endpoint:
GET /api/system-heartbeat

Returns:
- status
- timestamp
- process uptime
- agentPoolHealthy flag (if available)

Safety:
- Read-only
- No coupling to SSE or rendering layers
- Fully isolated addition

Result:
System now exposes a simple heartbeat for external monitoring and future expansion.
