# GOVERNANCE ADVISORY ENGINE MODEL
## Phase 277
## Governance Advisory Signal Generation Layer

---

## PURPOSE

Define how governance advisories are generated from passive governance signals.

This phase formalizes:

Signal classification
Advisory generation
Risk scoring
Operator presentation structure
Signal lifecycle rules

This phase remains advisory only.

No enforcement introduced.

---

## ADVISORY ENGINE PRINCIPLE

Governance advisory engine must:

Interpret governance signals
Classify governance observations
Produce operator advisories
Maintain execution isolation
Preserve operator authority

Advisory engine must never:

Control execution
Modify tasks
Influence routing
Alter agents
Trigger enforcement

Engine produces informational guidance only.

---

## SIGNAL CLASSIFICATION MODEL

Governance signals must classify into categories:

INFORMATIONAL
AWARENESS
BOUNDARY_NOTICE
RISK_OBSERVATION
SAFETY_NOTE

Classification must remain:

Deterministic
Consistent
Explainable
Auditable

Classification must never:

Trigger automated response.

---

## GOVERNANCE RISK SCORING APPROACH

Risk scoring may include levels:

NONE
LOW
MODERATE
ELEVATED
HIGH

Risk scoring must remain:

Advisory only
Operator visible
Non-binding
Non-executing

Risk scoring must never:

Trigger enforcement
Change execution behavior
Override operator judgment

---

## ADVISORY GENERATION STRUCTURE

Governance advisory must include:

Advisory reason
Observed condition
Governance boundary reference
Suggested awareness note
Risk classification
Operator visibility flag

Advisory must remain:

Readable
Deterministic
Traceable
Non-directive

Advisory must never instruct execution.

---

## OPERATOR ADVISORY FORMAT

Operator advisories should present:

What governance observed
Why governance noted it
What boundary applies
What governance cannot do
Operator remains decision authority

Advisories must remain:

Neutral
Informational
Non-directive
Transparent

---

## GOVERNANCE SIGNAL LIFECYCLE

Governance signals must follow lifecycle:

Signal created
Signal classified
Advisory generated
Operator visible
Signal archived

Signals must never:

Trigger execution behavior
Escalate automatically
Mutate system state

Lifecycle remains informational.

Governance advisory engine must remain:
