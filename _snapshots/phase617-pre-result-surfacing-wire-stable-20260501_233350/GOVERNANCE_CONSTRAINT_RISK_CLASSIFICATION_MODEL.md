# GOVERNANCE CONSTRAINT RISK CLASSIFICATION MODEL
## Phase 258

---

## PURPOSE

Define how governance constraints are classified by risk
without introducing enforcement authority.

Risk classification provides:

Operator awareness
Governance prioritization
Constraint understanding

Risk classification does NOT:

Block execution
Trigger enforcement
Alter lifecycle state

Risk classification is cognition only.

---

## RISK CLASSIFICATION PRINCIPLE

Constraint risk must be:

Deterministic
Explainable
Signal-derived
Operator visible

Risk must never be:

Implicit
Self-modifying
Opaque

Risk must always be explainable from signals.

---

## RISK TIER DEFINITIONS

Constraints must classify into tiers:

LOW_RISK  
MODERATE_RISK  
HIGH_RISK  
CRITICAL_RISK  

Optional future:

UNKNOWN_RISK (insufficient signals)

Risk tiers must never imply:

Automatic enforcement.

---

## RISK SCORING RULE

Risk scoring must derive from:

Signal reliability
Constraint scope
False positive potential
Operator impact
System impact

Risk score must be:

Deterministic
Reproducible
Signal explainable

Risk score must never be:

Heuristic-only
Opaque
Untraceable

---

## RISK VISIBILITY RULE

Risk classification must provide:

Risk tier
Risk score
Risk explanation
Signal basis
Confidence level

Operator must always see:

Why risk classification exists.

---

## OPERATOR RISK AWARENESS STRUCTURE

Risk outputs must prepare:

Operator review priority
Constraint attention signals
Governance focus indicators

Risk awareness must never:

Force operator action.

---

## MACHINE READABILITY RULE

Risk classification structures must include:

risk_id
constraint_id
risk_tier
risk_score
risk_basis_signals
risk_confidence
risk_last_updated
risk_visibility_state

Risk structures must remain:

Deterministic
Parseable
Versionable

---

## SAFETY GUARANTEE

Risk classification must remain:

Read-only
Deterministic
Authority preserving
Operator visible
Execution isolated

Risk classification must never:

Modify constraints
Trigger enforcement
Block execution
Change lifecycle state

Risk classification informs governance only.
