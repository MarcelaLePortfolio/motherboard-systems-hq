# Phase 402.3 — Governance Operator Decision Surface Model

## Purpose

Define how governance cognition supports operator decision making without becoming a decision engine, execution controller, or automation authority.

This defines decision support capability only.

Not decision execution.

---

## Capability Objective

The operator must be able to understand:

What governance determined
What it means operationally
What risks exist
What remains human judgment

This creates actionable understanding without removing authority.

---

## Decision Surface Definition

Decision surfaces must provide:

Interpretation summary
Operational meaning summary
Risk framing
Human judgment boundary
Investigation continuation paths

This is cognition-to-decision translation.

Not decision automation.

---

## Interpretation Summary

Must summarize:

Governance interpretation
Trust classification context
Stability expectations
Relevant cognition outcomes

This is the cognition anchor for decisions.

---

## Operational Meaning Summary

Must translate:

What the cognition implies
What it does not imply
What remains unknown
What remains unchanged

Operational meaning must remain neutral.

No recommendation language.

---

## Risk Framing

May identify:

Potential concern areas
Stability assurances
Uncertainty zones
Investigation opportunities

Risk framing must never:

Create urgency
Imply action required
Suggest execution steps

Risk is awareness only.

---

## Human Judgment Boundary

Decision surfaces must explicitly state:

Operator decides
System informs
Automation remains bounded
Trust remains informational

Authority clarity must always be preserved.

---

## Investigation Continuation Paths

Decision surfaces must allow navigation toward:

Evidence inspection
Historical interpretation
Trust explanation
Boundary models
Replay context

Decision understanding must remain inspectable.

---

## Explicit Prohibitions

Decision surfaces must never:

Recommend execution
Block execution
Trigger execution
Route work
Prioritize work
Act as policy engine

Decision support only.

---

## Safety Guarantees

This phase introduces:

No runtime behavior
No governance enforcement
No routing integration
No execution coupling
No reducer wiring

Capability definition only.

---

## Phase Classification

Phase 402.3 — Governance operator decision surface model

Classification:

Operator cognition decision-support capability
Governance consumption maturity expansion
No runtime capability change
