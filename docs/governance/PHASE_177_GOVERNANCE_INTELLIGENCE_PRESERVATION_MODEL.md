PURPOSE

Define preservation expectations for governance intelligence
to ensure governance knowledge remains stable, traceable,
and protected from unintended loss or drift.

PRESERVATION PRINCIPLE

Historically preserved
Terminologically stable
Structurally consistent
Non-executing
Authority aligned

Preservation protects governance continuity.

────────────────────────────────

PRESERVATION REQUIREMENTS

Governance intelligence documentation must:

Maintain prior phase records
Avoid silent removal of concepts

Maintain phase traceability
Protect governance reasoning lineage

Governance knowledge must remain durable.

Delete prior governance meaning without record
Remove governance phases silently
Break documentation traceability
Introduce undocumented replacements
Create governance knowledge gaps

Governance preservation is mandatory.
